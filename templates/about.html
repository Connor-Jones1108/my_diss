About the site

We are delighted to assist you in providing valuable insights and information about your living space. Please take a moment to fill out our detailed webform with the following details:

  • Address: Let us know where your property is located.
  • Postcode: This helps us identify the exact area for localized data.
  • Description: Share a brief description of your property. Feel free to mention any unique features or general observations.
  • Electricity Cost: How much do you pay for electricity? This helps us calculate average utility costs in your area.
  • Water Cost: Include your water expenses to aid in our cost analysis.
  • Internet Cost: Since connectivity is key, tell us about your internet service costs.
  • Council Tax: Input the amount you pay in council tax. This is important for budgeting and comparison.
  • Landlord Review: Provide feedback about your landlord. Your experience can help future tenants.
  • Property Tip: Do you have any tips or advice for living in your property or neighborhood? Share your insights!

Your information is invaluable in helping others make informed decisions about their rental choices. Thank you for your contribution!
